I am a MS student in the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech, under the guidance of Professor [Chris Thomas](https://people.cs.vt.edu/chris/), focuses on detecting disinformation. My research interests encompass AI, NLP, and CV. In the Virginia Tech, I am working on ["When Trust is a Factor"](https://topics.engineering.columbia.edu/when-trust-is-a-factor/) project, my work is mainly to develop the language models with RLHF and PPO. In addition, I worked on integrated the cross-modalities information in the knowledge graph to detect the disinformation. The goal is trying to figure out which tuples are real or fake to finish the fine-grained level entailment.  

I run an online scene representation reading list, where we discuss papers in 3D vision for the virtual and physical world. If you are interested, please visit the [reading list](https://github.com/tingchihc/scene-representation).
